‘PageTurner’ accessibility exercise

‘PageTurner’ is a fake service containing accessibility issues. Your task is to find as many of them as you can.

Instructions

Following the scope provided, we would like you to write a correctly formatted script using everything you have learned so far.

Just remember that first and foremost this is an opportunity for you to shine and to develop your confidence as a Screen Reader Analyst. Here at DAC we want you to be the best Analyst you can be because the work that you do will have a positive impact on Screen Reader users around the globe. This is nothing to stress over and you have the handouts if you need them. Good luck!

A world first

PageTurner is the only publisher, and a global publisher at that, who specialises in publishing accessible books. This specialism makes us extremely attractive to; authors, universities and other publishers. As a result, we provide unrivaled access to a wide range of genres including; academic works, a wide range of fiction, non-fiction and poetry. Given the quantity of material available to us, much of our publication is digital however, we can provide an on demand service.

About PageTurner

Founded in 2000, PageTurner has become a world leader in digital publishing. Moreover, for the past two decades, PageTurner has been at the forefront of publishing for the accessible market. This specialism allows us to produce the highest quality; Braille", large print and audio (opens in new window) publications. Our reputation also gives us access to the largest number of university libraries, commercial and independent publishing houses.

We work with partners from around the world
